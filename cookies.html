Clidder Cookies Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They help improve your experience by remembering your preferences and activity.

How We Use Cookies

At Clidder, we use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in and secure.
  • Personalize your experience on our platform.
  • Analyze site traffic and improve performance.
  • Deliver relevant advertising.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services to set cookies on your device to help us deliver ads and measure their effectiveness. These third parties have their own privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies via your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your experience and some features may not work properly.

Questions?

For any questions about our cookies policy, please contact us at privacy@clidder.com.
