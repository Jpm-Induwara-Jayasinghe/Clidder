Ads Information

Effective Date: May 29, 2025

Advertising helps keep Clidder free for everyone. This page explains how ads work on our platform, what data we use, and your control over ad experiences.

1. Why You See Certain Ads

We show ads based on your activity on Clidder, including your profile, interactions, and topics you follow. This helps us make ads more relevant to you.

2. What Data Is Used

We may use information such as your device type, location (if shared), and engagement with posts or hashtags to deliver more targeted ads.

3. Ad Preferences

You can customize your ad experience in your Settings > Ads Preferences. You can manage topics, advertisers, and whether we personalize ads based on your activity.

4. Third-Party Advertisers

Some ads may be delivered by third-party partners. These partners may collect limited information to measure performance or target ads across platforms.

5. Your Control

You can opt out of interest-based ads or request to see fewer ads about specific topics. You can also disable ad personalization entirely in your settings.

6. Sponsored Content

All paid ads are clearly labeled as “Promoted”. We do not sell your private messages or confidential content to advertisers.

7. Reporting Ads

If you find an ad that is inappropriate or misleading, you can report it directly using the “...” menu on the ad post.

8. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about ads on Clidder, contact us at ads@clidder.com.
