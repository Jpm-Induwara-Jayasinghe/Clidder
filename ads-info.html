Advertising on Clidder

Overview

Clidder offers a dynamic advertising platform tailored to connect your brand with our vibrant community. Whether you’re a startup or an established business, our ad solutions help you reach your audience effectively.

Ad Types Available

  • Promoted Posts: Amplify your message by promoting posts directly in users’ timelines.
  • Display Ads: Eye-catching banners and sidebars across the site with high visibility.
  • Video Ads: Engage users with short, impactful video advertisements.
  • Targeted Campaigns: Use demographic and interest data to reach the right audience.

Privacy and Data

We prioritize user privacy and comply with data protection regulations. Ads are served based on anonymous aggregate data. Clidder does not share personal data with advertisers.

All ad data and analytics are securely stored on Black Hat servers managed by Black Hat Company.
