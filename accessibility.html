Accessibility at Clidder

Our Commitment

At Clidder, we are committed to making our platform accessible to everyone, including users with disabilities. We continuously improve our design, development, and content to meet the highest accessibility standards.

Accessibility Features

  • High-contrast dark mode with orange accent for clear visibility.
  • Keyboard navigable interface ensuring full functionality without a mouse.
  • Screen reader friendly semantic HTML markup.
  • Clear focus indicators on interactive elements.
  • Text alternatives for images and icons.
  • Resizable text with no loss of content or functionality.
  • Consistent and simple layout for ease of use.

Standards and Compliance

Clidder strives to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. We regularly test and update our platform to ensure accessibility for all users.
