Clidder

Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: May 29, 2025

Clidder is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience and applying relevant accessibility standards.

1. Our Commitment

We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA wherever possible. We aim to make our platform inclusive and usable for everyone.

2. Accessibility Features

Clidder includes features such as:

  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Color contrast awareness
  • Resizable text options
  • Alt text for images and icons

3. Ongoing Efforts

We work with accessibility experts and conduct regular audits to identify and fix barriers. Accessibility is a core part of our product development process.

4. Contact Us

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of Clidder or have suggestions to improve accessibility, please contact us at accessibility@clidder.com.

5. Third-Party Content

While we make every effort to ensure accessibility, some third-party tools and content may not be fully accessible. We encourage providers to meet accessibility standards.
